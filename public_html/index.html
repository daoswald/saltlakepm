Salt Lake Perl Mongers

The Salt Lake City Perl Mongers meet monthly to revel in Perl and Perl-related topics. Meetings usually include a presentation, and there's always a social element. If you enjoy the Perl Programming Language, or would like to become more proficient with Perl, join us.

The Future
In October we will be featuring Lightning Talks. These are five or ten minute talks, one after another for 60 to 90 minutes total, from anyone who wishes to present something of interest to Perl Mongers. If you would like to participate, send an email on the mailing list.
